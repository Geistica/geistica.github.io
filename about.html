About Me

Hello. I'm Samuel — a digital creator blending IT, graphics, and VFX with a taste for clean, minimal, retro-futuristic interfaces.

I build static, high-performance sites with an artistic backbone. My aesthetic lives somewhere between brutalist structure and conceptual UI minimalism. This portfolio is a curated expression of that ethos.

Beyond code, I explore design systems, visual abstraction, and synth-driven horror atmospheres — whether in motion graphics, sound design, or interactive architecture.
